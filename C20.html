Facts & Questions

What is the active ingredient?

Our key ingredient is Hypochlorous Acid (HOCL). Hypochlorous Acid is a compound produced in the body’s white blood cells and used to remove bacteria and viruses. Think of Chapter 20 as a second helping.

Is it safe and effective?

Absolutely! Chapter 20 is FDA-approved and dermatologist-adored because it is proven to remove viruses and bacteria.

What is an antimicrobial?

Antibacterial solutions remove bacteria, that’s it. Antimicrobials remove bacteria, mold, fungi, pollution, and even viruses (cough Covid cough) and create a barrier preventing regrowth.
